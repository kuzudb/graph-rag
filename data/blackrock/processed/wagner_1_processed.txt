Susan Wagner is a founder and director of asset manager BlackRock Inc. BlackRock Inc. was started with Larry Fink and others in 1988. Wagner founded the company at age 26. Wagner served as chief operating officer and vice chairman of BlackRock Inc. Wagner oversaw BlackRock Inc.'s 2009 merger with Barclay's Global Investors. This merger transformed BlackRock Inc. into the world's largest asset manager.

Prior to founding BlackRock Inc. Wagner worked as a vice president of the mortgage finance group at Lehman Brothers. Wagner retired in 2012. Wagner remains on the board of BlackRock Inc. Wagner also serves as a director of Apple and startups Color Health and Samsara.
