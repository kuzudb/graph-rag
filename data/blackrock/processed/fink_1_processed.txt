Larry Fink is the Founder Chairman and CEO of BlackRock Inc. Larry Fink and seven partners founded BlackRock Inc. in 1988. Under Larry Fink's leadership BlackRock Inc. has grown into a global leader in investment and technology solutions to help investors build better financial futures. Today BlackRock Inc. is trusted to manage 10 trillion dollars in assets more than any other investment firm in the world.

Larry Fink was born on November 2 1952 in Van Nuys is a city in California. Larry Fink earned an MBA from UCLA in 1976 and a BA in political science also from UCLA in 1974.
